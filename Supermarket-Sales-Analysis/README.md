# Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes supermarket sales performance, customer segments, product categories, regional sales distribution, shipping trends, and overall business growth. The dashboard helps identify top-performing products, customer behavior patterns, and geographical sales opportunities.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

---

## Key KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Customer Segments
- Regional Performance

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Overview

Key analysis includes:

- Overall Sales Performance
- Profit Analysis
- Order Trends
- Customer Segment Performance
- Category-wise Sales Distribution
- Business Growth Metrics

### 2. Customer & Product Analysis

Key analysis includes:

- Top Products by Sales
- Category & Sub-Category Performance
- Customer Segment Contribution
- Product Sales Distribution
- Yearly Sales Trends
- Product-Level Insights

### 3. Geography & Shipping Insights

Key analysis includes:

- Regional Sales Comparison
- Top States by Sales
- Monthly Order Trends
- Shipping Mode Analysis
- Geographic Performance Evaluation
- Logistics Insights

---

## Key Business Insights

- Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue share.
- Technology category generates the strongest sales performance.
- Phones and Chairs are among the top-performing products.
- West region records the highest sales volume.
- Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode.
- Sales growth accelerated significantly in later years.
- Revenue is concentrated among a few high-performing states.

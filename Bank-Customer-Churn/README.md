# Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn behavior in a banking environment. The objective is to identify customer segments with higher churn risk and understand the key factors influencing customer retention.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

## Key KPIs

- Total Customers: 10,000
- Retained Customers: 7,963
- Churned Customers: 2,037
- Churn Rate: 20.4%
- Average Credit Score: 651
- Active Members: 51.5%
- Average Balance: 76.5K
- Average Salary: 100K

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Customer Churn Overview

This page provides a high-level overview of customer attrition patterns.

Key analyses include:

- Churned Customers by Geography
- Churned Customers by Age Group
- Churned Customers by Gender
- Churned Customers by Credit Band
- Customer Distribution by Number of Products

### 2. Customer Risk & Retention Analysis

This page focuses on factors influencing customer retention and churn risk.

Key analyses include:

- Churned Customers by Tenure
- Churned Customers by Active Membership Status
- Average Balance by Customer Status
- Churned Customers by Credit Band
- Product-wise Churn Analysis

## Business Insights

- Germany records the highest customer churn among all regions.
- Customers aged 40–49 show the highest churn rate.
- Inactive customers contribute significantly to overall churn.
- Customers holding only one product are more likely to leave.
- Credit score and customer engagement are major churn indicators.
- High account balances alone do not guarantee customer retention.

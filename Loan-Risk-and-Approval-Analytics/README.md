# Loan Risk & Approval Analytics

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes loan applications, approval trends, applicant profiles, and risk indicators. The objective is to understand factors influencing loan approval decisions and identify high-risk applicants.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

## Key KPIs

- Total Applications: 4,269
- Approved Loans: 2,656
- Rejected Loans: 1,613
- Approval Rate: 62.2%
- Average Credit Score: 599.9
- Average Income: 5.1M
- Average Loan Amount: 15.1M

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Loan Risk & Approval Overview

This page provides a summary of loan applications and approval performance.

Key analyses include:

- Approval vs Rejection Rate
- Applications by Employment Status
- Applicant Distribution by Education
- Average Credit Score
- Average Income and Loan Amount

### 2. Risk Analysis

This page focuses on borrower risk assessment and credit quality.

Key analyses include:

- Credit Score Analysis
- High Credit vs Low Credit Applicants
- Average CIBIL by Loan Status
- Applications by Loan Term
- Asset Composition Analysis
- Education-wise Approval Trends

## Business Insights

- Overall loan approval rate stands at 62.2%.
- Applicants with stronger credit profiles are more likely to receive approval.
- Graduate and non-graduate applicants show similar application volumes.
- Loan term significantly impacts application distribution.
- Asset ownership provides additional indicators for risk assessment.
- Credit score remains one of the strongest drivers of approval decisions.

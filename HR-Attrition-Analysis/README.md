# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes employee attrition patterns, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, work-life balance, salary trends, and department performance to identify key factors influencing employee turnover.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

---

## Key KPIs

- Total Employees: 1470
- Average Age: 36.9
- Average Salary: $6.5K
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.1%

---

---

## Key Business Insights

- Employees in lower salary bands show higher attrition rates.
- Overtime is strongly associated with employee turnover.
- Research & Development department contributes the highest attrition volume.
- Employees with low job satisfaction are more likely to leave.
- Attrition decreases as employee tenure increases.
- Travel frequency impacts employee retention.
- Work-life balance plays a significant role in workforce stability.
